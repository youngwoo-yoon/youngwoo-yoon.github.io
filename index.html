About
Youngwoo Yoon is a Principal Researcher in the Social Robotics Research Lab at ETRI (Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute), South Korea, and an Assistant Professor at UST (University of Science and Technology). His work bridges HCI, robotics, and machine learning. He's especially interested in developing autonomous social agents.
